Scope

Three coordinated deliverables

The pilot remains intentionally narrow. Floor-plan completion outranks automation, software development, and broader permit-document production.

01

Existing floor plan

Measured existing-condition plan developed from the controlling project sources, with material conflicts documented rather than concealed.

02

Proposed floor plan

Rear addition, revised living/kitchen planning, and deck relationship coordinated to BDPC conceptual direction.

03

Site and area plan

Residence footprint, proposed work, deck/porch context, and agreed area information without implying survey services.

  • Native AutoCAD DWGs and coordinated PDF sets
