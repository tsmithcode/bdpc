06 · Draft acceptance criteria

What a successful pilot should prove

These criteria convert the call into a reviewable production standard. Brian can confirm or revise them after the source audit and before written authorization.

Scope

Three defined sheets

The returned package covers only the three sheets Brian identified, with exact numbers, titles, and included content confirmed from the uploaded set.

File continuity

BDPC can open and continue the work

Native DWGs, referenced files, fonts, plot styles, title-block assets, and a dependency manifest are organized so BDPC is not locked into CAD Guardian.

Graphic standard

Current BDPC paper-space presentation

Sheets use the approved BDPC title block, paper-space organization, plotting behavior, annotation, and current graphic conventions.

Dimensional standard

Rational, self-checking geometry

Dimensions display to 1/2-inch precision, overall and chained dimensions reconcile, and the 3.5-inch framed-wall base convention is used unless verified conditions require otherwise.

Source fidelity

No invented conditions

Doors, windows, walls, deck/addition geometry, and site relationships derive from the approved files, LiDAR, floor plate, or explicit BDPC direction; unresolved conditions are flagged.

Review package

Clear first review

BDPC receives a review PDF, agreed native files, an assumption/issue log, and a concise list of items requiring Brian’s decision.

Commercial and schedule criteria remain open: turnaround, included review cycles, rate/fee, payment terms, and final issue date will be established only after the complete source package is audited.
