Included Work

Produce one existing main-level as-built floor plan from the controlling point cloud/supporting package. Excluded Work

No proposed plan, site plan, other floors, elevations, sections, BIM/3D, schedules, design services, survey, field verification, permit/professional certification, engineering, code/zoning analysis, added review rounds, or work beyond this one-sheet assignment.

Drafting Standards

Use BDPC-confirmed preferences: dimensions no finer than 1/2 inch unless directed; 3.5 inch typical framed-wall baseline; TCADD floor-plan/model-space logic; 1419/BDPC reference for presentation, title block, text, dimensions, line hierarchy, and notation.

Point clouds, images, PDFs, DWGs, DXFs, and reference packages are supporting evidence, not independent truth. Do not invent unsupported room names, hidden conditions, dimensions, openings, or assemblies. Disclose unresolved conditions.

Acceptance requires readable plotting, clean main-level geometry, no unexplained duplicate/gap conditions in traced walls, supported openings and labels, and disclosure of unresolved scale/source conflicts. Payment is collected after delivery.
