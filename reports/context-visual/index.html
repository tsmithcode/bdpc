Interior, entry, and capture context

These companion contact sheets connect photographic capture context to scan previews and help the drafter interpret room, entry, opening, circulation, and capture-location evidence.

Drafting use and decision rules

  • Use for contextReview visible massing, access, openings, stairs, deck/porch relationships, room character, and capture coverage.
  • Verify geometryDo not scale photographs or substitute appearance for confirmed dimensions and controlling CAD.
  • Normalize orientationCoordinate every photographic or scan observation to the recorded project-north and source-view rotation controls.
  • Maintain truth boundaryThe images do not establish survey accuracy, registered scans, concealed conditions, code compliance, or final production.
