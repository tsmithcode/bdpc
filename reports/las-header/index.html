Validation method

  1. Confirm readable LAS structure, version, point format, dimensions, scales, and offsets.
  2. Compare recorded source and working point counts and metadata.
  3. Read deterministic stride samples plus bounded first and last point windows.
  4. Confirm working bounds remain contained by source bounds and endpoint expectations.
  5. Match recorded checksums and manifest entries without writing to the confidential source tree.

Interpretation rules and limits

  • What passedFile-level integrity and the recorded structural/sampling controls for five source/working pairs.
  • What did not passNo accepted registration tolerance, survey test, controlling-design comparison, or licensed CAD production test.
  • UnitsCandidate metre interpretation remains an analytical control pending reconciliation with controlling project information.
  • CoordinatesExact coordinates and detailed bounds remain private; no coordinate transform was applied for this report.
