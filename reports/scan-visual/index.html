How to read these figures

  • Plan densityShows where points accumulate in the native/local XY view; density is not a wall-line or survey determination.
  • Intensity planDisplays relative return-intensity structure and may reveal material or capture variation; it is not photographic color.
  • Elevation densityXZ and YZ projections expose vertical coverage and outliers without applying alignment transforms.
  • Height distributionSummarizes point frequency by candidate vertical coordinate; floor and unit interpretation remain analytical controls.
